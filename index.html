Who Are We

A Bunch Of Astromaniacs!

We are the Astronomy and Space Technology Club at IIIT Hyderabad, a vibrant community united by a shared passion for exploring the universe. From stargazing sessions to star parties, hands-on workshops and exciting projects, we bring space science to life for enthusiasts of all levels.

We have undertaken exciting projects and participated in numerous National and International competitions, gaining experience and recognition along the way. Join along in our journey into the fascinating world of Astronomy and SpaceTech!
