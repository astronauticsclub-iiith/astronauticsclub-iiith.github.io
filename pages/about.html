History behind the club

The idea of starting an Astronomy club struck one of the founders on Himalaya block terrace where Prof. Praveen Paruchuri had invited everyone to witness the ‚ÄúSuper Blue Blood Moon‚Äù - the lunar eclipse on 31st January 2018. Six freshmen: Yash Chaurasia, Jayadev Naram, Prajwal Krishna Maitin, Tanmay Kumar Sinha, Siddharth Jain and Jainam Khakhra decided to take this idea seriously and mailed Prof. Subhadip Mitra for support/mentorship. His reply? He was waiting for someone in the college to take interest in this field.

With support from Prof. Radhika Mamidi then the SLC chair, an 8 inch Dobosonian reflector telescope was procured. The first Astronomy club event was a total Lunar eclipse viewing session on 27th July 2018 - the start of a new academic year and our club as well! Further equipments like binoculars, different eyepieces, tripods etc. were procured in the following year enhancing observational capabilities. A special mention goes to Ansh Puvvada and Shreyas Badami, who were critical managed the club in the following years, despite the halt in the club‚Äôs expansion due to COVID.

Around this time, India‚Äôs space sector was witnessing a remarkable boom, marked by the emergence of multiple SpaceTech startups and the successful Chandrayaan-3 mission. Riding this wave of enthusiasm, the club expanded its focus to include SpaceTech, leading to notable achievements in 2021 and 2024, when our teams reached the World Finals of the prestigious CanSat Competition‚Äîa miniature satellite design challenge organized by the American Astronautical Society and NASA. In July 2024, the club was officially renamed as Astronautics Club, reflecting our expanded vision and aspirations.

What kind of activities does the club organize?
We organize a mix of Astronomy and Aeronautics themed events. Some of regular activities include:
  • Stargazing sessions
  • Starparty trips at the city outskirts
  • Astrophotography nights
  • Astronomy theory sessions
  • Computational Astronomy competition like Exoplanet hunt
  • Workshops on CAD Modelling and Model Rocketry
  • Internal club projects
